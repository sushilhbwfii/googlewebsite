About Sushil Subedi

A Legacy of Trust and Precision — Your Trusted Chartered Accountant in Pokhara, Nepal

Dedicated to Financial Excellence

I am Sushil Subedi, a Chartered Accountant and Business Consultant based in Lakeside, Pokhara, Nepal. My journey in the world of finance and accounting has been driven by a singular mission: to provide businesses and individuals with the highest quality financial services underpinned by integrity, accuracy, and deep industry insight.

With years of hands-on experience across a broad spectrum of industries — from manufacturing and healthcare to hospitality, microfinance, retail, logistics, and non-profit organizations including United Nations projects — I bring a comprehensive understanding of diverse financial landscapes.

My practice is built on a client-first philosophy. I believe that every business is unique and deserves tailored financial solutions. Whether you need meticulous bookkeeping, strategic tax planning, accurate financial statements, or full-spectrum accounting support, I am committed to helping you achieve financial clarity and operational success.

Over the years, 900+ clients have trusted me with their financial management, and I take that responsibility seriously. My approach combines rigorous professional standards with a warm, approachable style — making complex financial concepts accessible and manageable for every client.
